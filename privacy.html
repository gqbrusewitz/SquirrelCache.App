Privacy Policy

How SquirrelCache handles your data

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

SquirrelCache is built on a simple principle: your photos are yours, and nobody else's business. This privacy policy is refreshingly short because we genuinely don't collect, process, or have access to any of your data.

Data Collection

We collect zero data. SquirrelCache does not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Track your usage or behavior
  • Use analytics or telemetry
  • Access or view your photos
  • Store any of your data on our servers
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Require you to create an account

How the App Works

SquirrelCache acts as a pass-through tool using Apple's native iOS APIs. When you export photos:

  1. The app uses Apple's PhotoKit framework to access your photos locally on your device
  2. Files are copied directly from your device to your chosen destination
  3. Everything happens on your device - nothing is sent to our servers
  4. We never see, process, or have access to your photos

Third-Party Services

SquirrelCache does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app operates entirely offline and independently.

Data Storage

We don't store your data because we never receive it in the first place. Your photos remain:

  • On your device
  • In your chosen backup locations
  • Completely under your control

Children's Privacy

SquirrelCache does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data about you, there's nothing for us to:

  • Share with third parties
  • Sell or monetize
  • Delete or modify
  • Export or transfer

Your privacy is protected by design, not just by policy.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data practices (which would require fundamentally changing how the app works), we will update this policy and notify users through the App Store update notes.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or SquirrelCache's privacy practices, please contact:

SquirrelCacheApp@gmail.com

Legal Requirements

In the unlikely event we are required by law to provide information, we would have nothing to provide, as we genuinely don't collect or have access to user data.

The Bottom Line: SquirrelCache is designed so that we couldn't access your data even if we tried to. Your photos, your backups, your privacy.
